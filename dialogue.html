Nobel Week Dialogue

The Nobel Week Dialogue is a free of charge event that is part of the official Nobel Week programme.

The event aims to stimulate discussion at the highest level on a topical science-related theme by bringing together Nobel Prize laureates, the world’s leading scientists and experts, key opinion leaders, policy makers and the general public, online as well as on site. By bridging science and society, it’s an opportunity to stimulate thinking, excite imagination and inspire greatness.
